Delete Your Dharma Account

Submit a request to permanently delete your account and associated data.

How to Request Deletion

To initiate the deletion of your Dharma account, please follow these steps:

  • Send an email from your registered email address (the one you used to sign up for the app).
  • Address the email to: Itcoreeluru@gmail.com
  • Include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line.
  • Clearly state your request for permanent account and data deletion in the email body.

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon processing your request, all personal data associated with your account will be permanently removed from our systems, including:

  • User Profile Information: Name, contact details, and account settings.
  • Petition Data: All records of petitions submitted by you.
  • Uploaded Files: Documents, images, or any other media attached to your petitions.
  • Notification Tokens: Device tokens used for push notifications.
  • Personal Data: Any other personal information stored within the Dharma app infrastructure.

Deletion Timeline

Our team will manually verify and process your deletion request. This process is typically completed within 7 working days of receiving your email.

Important Notice

Warning: The account deletion process is permanent. Once completed, your account, petitions, and uploaded data cannot be recovered. If you wish to use Dharma in the future, you will need to create a new account.
