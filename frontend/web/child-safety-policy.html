Child Safety Standards Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Child Safety

Dharma is committed to maintaining a safe and secure platform for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy toward any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Protecting children and preventing misuse of our platform is a top priority.

2. Prohibited Content and Behavior

Users are strictly prohibited from creating, uploading, sharing, transmitting, or distributing any content that:

  • Exploits or abuses minors in any way.
  • Contains sexual content involving minors.
  • Promotes, facilitates, or depicts child exploitation.
  • Endangers the safety or well-being of children.

Enforcement: Any violation of these rules will result in immediate action, including removal of content and suspension or permanent termination of user accounts.

3. Compliance with Laws and Authorities

Dharma complies with all applicable child protection laws and regulations. We cooperate fully with law enforcement authorities and relevant organizations in investigating and addressing cases of suspected child exploitation or abuse.

4. Reporting Mechanism

We encourage users to report any content or behavior that may violate this policy or endanger children.

Reports can be submitted by contacting us at:

Email: coordinator_skillcensus@rgukt.in

Please include relevant details such as user information, screenshots, or descriptions to help us investigate effectively.

5. Review and Enforcement

Upon receiving a report:

  • Our team will review the content promptly.
  • Violating content will be removed immediately.
  • Appropriate actions will be taken against the responsible accounts, including suspension or permanent banning.
  • In serious cases, we may report incidents to law enforcement authorities.

6. Account Actions

Accounts found to violate child safety policies may face:

  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent account termination
  • Reporting to legal authorities where required

7. Commitment to a Safe Environment

Dharma is dedicated to creating a responsible and secure digital environment. We continuously improve our monitoring and safety practices to protect users, especially minors, from harm.

8. Contact Information

For safety concerns, questions, or reports related to this policy, please contact:

Email: coordinator_skillcensus@rgukt.in
